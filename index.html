Privacy Policy for TalkWrite

TalkWrite is a voice and text utility app that allows users to record audio, convert speech to text and vice versa, and manage audio/text content locally on their devices.

Information Collection and Use

TalkWrite does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. All recordings, text, and files are processed and stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to any server.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Microphone: To record voice input.
  • Storage: To save and access recordings and text files locally.

These permissions are used solely for core app functionality and not for data collection.

No Account or Login Required

Users are not required to create an account or log in. TalkWrite functions fully offline and does not connect to any external server or database.

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties. Sharing features are user-initiated (e.g., using device-native sharing functions) and are not tracked or recorded by the app.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, updates will be posted on this page.
